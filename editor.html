Title (if you click, you can edit the name)

if you click on the mouse icon, the user can select, move, and resize content in the collage.

if you click on the square, you can select an image to have as the background or color.

if you right click on content, you have options to cut, copy, duplicate, or re-shape (go back to the cutting board).

If you click on content and the cutting board is empty, it will show up on the cutting board.

If you click on content and the cutting board is not empty, it will ask you if you want to replace the existing content and show up on the cutting board.

If press on the scroll button and drag it down, it will scroll through the content.

The cursor becomes a scissor when it is within the bounds of the cutting board.

When you press down and drag, the cutting tool starts cutting the photo's outsides that are bisected by the tool.

When you are done cutting, there will be a button to export it to the canvas.

If you click on the export button, the content will appear on the canvas where you can arrange it.
